# ZEALWISH

> Create. Grow. Own your AI character.

ZEALWISH is an AI character platform where anyone can create, grow, and truly own a living AI companion. It is not a productivity assistant, not only a chatbot, and not a speculative NFT shell. It is a character life system: visual identity, personality, memory, relationship growth, and portable ownership.

## Core Idea

**Free will for your digital self.**

ZEALWISH treats an AI character as a long-term digital being shaped by the user. The character remembers moments, grows through interaction, and becomes part of the user's digital identity.

## Why Web3

**Ownership is the product.**

NFT is not the product. Ownership is. When an AI character carries a user's memories, taste, relationship history, and identity, it should not be locked inside one platform database forever.

Web3 supports the product only where it strengthens the experience:

- **Identity:** a persistent character profile owned by the user.
- **Memory:** durable records that preserve relationship continuity.
- **Portability:** a future path for the character to move across apps, games, creator worlds, and agent networks.
- **Creator economy:** skins, lore, scenes, and character extensions can become user-owned assets.

## Product Pillars

| Pillar | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Create Your OC | Generate and shape a visual AI character with personality, voice, and style. |
| Grow Through Memory | Turn daily conversations and relationship moments into a structured memory timeline. |
| Own the Identity | Represent the character identity as a portable ownership layer. |
| Carry Across Worlds | Prepare the character for future games, social spaces, creator markets, and agent worlds. |

## Audience

ZEALWISH is for everyone who wants an AI character that feels personally theirs:

- ordinary users
- game players
- virtual character fans
- creators
- lonely people
- long-term companion seekers
- digital identity users
- people who want to bring their AI character across platforms
